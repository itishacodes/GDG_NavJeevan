Government Health Schemes


Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY)


• Provides free health insurance of up to ₹5 lakh per family per year for economically weaker sections.

• Covers hospitalization costs for secondary and tertiary care.

Know More

Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana (RSBY)


• Offers health insurance of ₹30,000 per family for Below Poverty Line (BPL) families.

• Covers hospitalization and medical expenses for enrolled beneficiaries.

Know More

Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)


• Accident insurance scheme providing coverage of up to ₹2 lakh for accidental death or disability.

• Premium: Only ₹20 per year per person.

Know More

Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY)


• Aimed at promoting institutional deliveries for pregnant women.

• Provides financial assistance to pregnant women, especially in rural areas, to encourage safer childbirth.

Know More

Mission Indradhanush


• A nationwide vaccination program ensuring immunization for children under 2 years and pregnant women.

• Covers diseases like polio, tuberculosis, measles, and hepatitis B.
