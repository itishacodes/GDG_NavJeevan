Government Health Schemes


Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY)


• Provides free health insurance of up to ₹5 lakh per family per year for economically weaker sections.

• Covers hospitalization costs for secondary and tertiary care.

Know More

Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana (RSBY)


• Offers health insurance of ₹30,000 per family for Below Poverty Line (BPL) families.

• Covers hospitalization and medical expenses for enrolled beneficiaries.

Know More

Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)


• Accident insurance scheme providing coverage of up to ₹2 lakh for accidental death or disability.

• Premium: Only ₹20 per year per person.

Know More

Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY)


• Aimed at promoting institutional deliveries for pregnant women.

• Provides financial assistance to pregnant women, especially in rural areas, to encourage safer childbirth.

Know More

Mission Indradhanush


• A nationwide vaccination program ensuring immunization for children under 2 years and pregnant women.

• Covers diseases like polio, tuberculosis, measles, and hepatitis B.

Government Education Schemes


Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA)


• Aims at universalizing education from preschool to Class 12.

• Focuses on school infrastructure, teacher training, and digital learning.

Know More

Mid-Day Meal Scheme (MDM)


• Provides free nutritious meals to school children in government and government-aided schools.

• Helps in reducing malnutrition and increasing school attendance.

Know More

National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship (NMMS)


• Scholarship for meritorious students from economically weaker backgrounds.

• Students of Class 8th can apply and receive ₹12,000 per year.

Know More

Pradhan Mantri Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (PM-USHA)


• Focuses on higher education by improving infrastructure in universities and colleges.

• Encourages research, innovation, and skill-based education.

Know More

SWAYAM (Study Webs of Active Learning for Young Aspiring Minds)


• Free online learning platform offering courses from top universities.

• Provides certifications recognized in higher education and jobs.

Government Finance Schemes


Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY)


• Financial inclusion scheme providing zero-balance bank accounts with debit cards.

• Offers ₹2 lakh accident insurance and ₹30,000 life cover for eligible beneficiaries.

Know More

Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY)


• Provides collateral-free loans up to ₹10 lakh for small businesses and startups.

• Loans categorized into Shishu (up to ₹50,000), Kishor (₹50,000 - ₹5 lakh), and Tarun (₹5-10 lakh).

Know More

Atal Pension Yojana (APY)


• A pension scheme for workers in the unorganized sector.

• Provides a fixed pension of ₹1,000 to ₹5,000 per month after retirement.

Know More

Stand-Up India Scheme


• Provides loans between ₹10 lakh to ₹1 crore to SC/ST and women entrepreneurs.

• Supports new business ventures in manufacturing, services, and trading sectors.

Know More

Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY)


• A savings scheme for the girl child, offering high interest rates and tax benefits.

• Parents can invest for a girl below 10 years with partial withdrawal for higher education.

Know More



Government Skill Development Schemes


Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)


• Provides free skill training with industry-recognized certification.

• Focuses on increasing employability through technical and vocational training.

Know More

Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY)


• Aims at training rural youth between 15-35 years for job-oriented skills.

• Provides placement support in various industries after training.

Know More

National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS)


• Encourages apprenticeships by offering financial incentives to industries.

• Supports on-the-job training for youth in various sectors.

Know More

Skill India Mission


• An umbrella initiative aimed at equipping youth with technical and soft skills.

• Provides training in multiple domains to enhance employment opportunities.

Know More

Startup India Learning Program


• Online training program to help entrepreneurs develop business and finance skills.

• Offers free courses with certifications to support startups and innovation.
