Master essential digital skills for a connected world

What to Learn? 📌

Basic Digital Skills

Learn how to use smartphones, email, and social media effectively.

Online Business & E-commerce

Discover how to sell products on Amazon, Etsy, and Facebook Marketplace.

Financial Management

Master budgeting, savings, and access government schemes.

How to Learn? 📚

📱 Take Online Courses
Enroll in digital literacy courses on platforms like Coursera, Udemy, and Khan Academy. They provide structured learning and certifications.
🧠 Practice Daily
Use digital tools like email, payments, and social media in your daily life to build skills with regular practice.
🏫 Join Workshops
Attend nearby training camps by local NGOs, schools, or government initiatives. Learning with peers is fun and effective.
🤝 Engage in Community Learning
Be part of a local or online group where people help each other with doubts and share tips about using digital tools.
🏢 Use Government & NGO Programs
Explore programs like PMGDISHA and Digital India that offer free basic computer and smartphone usage training.
